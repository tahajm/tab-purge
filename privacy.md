# Privacy Policy for TabPurge

Last updated: June 7, 2026

TabPurge does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

The extension stores your domain list locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API (`chrome.storage.local`). This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to any server or third party.

The `tabs` permission is used solely to read tab URLs for matching and to close tabs you explicitly request. Tab URLs are never stored, logged, or transmitted.
